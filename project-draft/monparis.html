Mon Paris, YSL

Discover Mon Paris by YSL

 

A modern and romantic fragrance with a sweet and fruity opening of strawberry and raspberry. The floral heart of datura flower and peony adds a touch of femininity, while the white musk and patchouli base provide depth and sensuality. Mon Paris is a captivating and passionate scent, embodying a contemporary interpretation of love in the city of Paris.
